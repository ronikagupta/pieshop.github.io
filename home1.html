Our history

pie shop is a take away (fast food) outlet specialising in pies, especially meat pies.
Common in some parts of the United Kingdom and Australia.[1] A form of specialised bakery, or in more recent times purely a retail outlet selling reheated cooked pies,
pie shops in Australia usually sell meat pies, sausage rolls, pasties and other pastry-wrapped baked goods.
The growth of hot bread shops (direct sale bakeries, often franchises) in Australia has largely supplanted the specialist pie shop.
